Story

You are a camper, who got lost and couldn’t find his campsite; lucky for you, you see a mansion out in the distance, so you decide to spend the night there. The mansion is pretty old, probably made in the 1960’s, but you proceed anyway, you open the door and see a grand hall. But something seems off about the house, you look to the right, and you see a MONSTER!!!! 
You have to get out of the house asap! You find a map in the hall so decide to take it, NOW GET OUT OF THE HOUSE FAST!!!!



How to use:


Type commands like go[north/south/east/west] to go to different rooms in the mansion.
Type get[item name] to get the item in the room your in (just to let you know, that this will only work if you are in a room and it says you see a [item] otherwise you cant get it )

Just so you know, you might have to type a command twice, for it to work, But if it still doesn't work, then try these
